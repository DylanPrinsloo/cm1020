1. Reflexive
Everything is related to itself

2. Symmetric  
If A is related to B, then B is related to A

3. Anti-symmetric
If one thing is related to another, and the other is related back the same way, then they must be the same thing

4. Transitive
If one thing is related to a second thing, and that second thing is related to a third thing, then the first thing is also related to the third thing

Quick Check
- Reflexive: All (a,a) pairs present?
- Symmetric: Every pair has its reverse?
- Anti-symmetric: No crossing pairs (except same element)?
- Transitive: All chains complete?
